About The Utah Core Room

UC
Utah educator & resource creator
Currently specializing in RISE test prep for grades 3-6

I started The Utah Core Room because I couldn't find what I needed. When I looked for RISE ELA practice materials that actually matched the test format — with original passages, the right question types, and complete answer keys — they didn't exist. So I built them myself.

Every resource in my TpT store is designed from the ground up for Utah Core Standards. I don't adapt generic materials and slap a "RISE" label on them. Every passage is original. Every question mirrors what students will actually see. Every product includes complete answer keys because I know you don't have time to grade another thing from scratch.

This blog is where I share the teaching strategies, wellness tips, and honest reflections that don't fit into a product listing. It's written for teachers who are doing their best in a system that doesn't always make it easy — and for parents who want to support their kids without adding more stress to an already full plate.

If you're a Utah elementary teacher preparing students for RISE testing, or a parent trying to understand what this whole testing thing is about, you're in the right place.
